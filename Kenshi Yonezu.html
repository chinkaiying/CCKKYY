About Kenshi Yonezu

Kenshi Yonezu is one of the most influential and innovative artists in Japan today. With his emotive songwriting, genre-defying sound, and ability to blend pop and experimental music, he has established himself as a leading figure in the modern Japanese music scene. His music continues to inspire and connect with fans across the world, making him a central figure in contemporary music.
